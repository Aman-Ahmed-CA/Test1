CA Rajesh Lihala

Founder & Managing Partner (FCA)

Mr. Rajesh Lihala, Independent Director of the Company, is a Fellow Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India and holds qualifications of B.Sc., FCA, DISA, and IP. He has completed CAAT (ICAI) in 2005. With vast experience in Audit, Taxation, and Insolvency, he is also a registered Resolution Professional with IBBI and has completed the Certificate Course in Concurrent Audit of Banks from ICAI. He currently serves as a Member of the Clinical Research Ethics Committee of Antara Psychiatric Hospital and Joint Secretary of the NGO Nish-Shabd Angeekar Welfare Association. He has earlier contributed to ICAI as a Co-opted Member of the Ethical Standards Committee (2011) and Special Invitee to the Board of Studies (2013–14).

CA Ajay Gupta

Partner (FCA, CS)

CA Ajay Gupta is a Fellow Chartered Accountant and also a qualified Company Secretary. With almost a decade of professional experience, he specializes in Direct and Indirect Taxation, corporate compliance, and advisory services. His dual qualification enables him to provide a unique blend of taxation and secretarial insights, making him an asset for businesses navigating today’s complex legal and fiscal environment. Ajay has guided numerous clients through intricate compliance matters and continues to deliver strategic value with practical solutions.

CA Sumit Kumar Mondal

Partner (ACA, NISM)

CA Sumit Kumar Mondal is an Associate Chartered Accountant and a recognized specialist in GST and Indirect Taxation. He also holds a prestigious NISM Certification, strengthening his capabilities in financial and securities market regulations. With extensive experience in international taxation, structuring, and compliance, Sumit has guided businesses in achieving tax efficiency while ensuring strict regulatory adherence. His combined expertise in GST and financial markets allows him to provide well-rounded, practical solutions for clients across industries.
